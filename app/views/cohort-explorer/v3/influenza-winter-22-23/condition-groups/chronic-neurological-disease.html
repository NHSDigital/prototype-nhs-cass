Flu vaccinations 2022 to 2023

Chronic neurological disease (flu 2022 to 2023)

CaaS digital pathway rule logic

The table below shows the digital cohort rules to determine who should be contacted or reminded to receive their flu vaccination based on specific medical conditions.

These conditions are detailed in influenza Green Book, chapter 19.

Condition description Ruleset Datasets used
Condition description

Conditions in which respiratory function may be compromised due to neurological disease (for example, people with polio syndrome)

Clinicians should offer immunisation, based on individual assessment, to clinically vulnerable individuals.

This includes those with:

  • cerebral palsy
  • learning disabilities
  • multiple sclerosis and related or similar conditions
  • hereditary and degenerative disease of the nervous system or muscles
  • severe neurological disability
  • stroke or transient ischaemic attack

Stroke, transient ischaemic attack (TIA).

 Ruleset

People over 6 months of age and recorded with any of the following:

  • a learning disability
  • chronic neurological disease

Conditions that may not be included in the CaaS digital pathway
