Cohort background

The Public Health Commissioning Office (PHCO) have commissioned the NHS England Vaccine Delivery Service (VDS) to support an urgent national MMR catch-up campaign over the course of the next 12 months, partly in response to the current measles outbreak in London. All individuals of the eligible age range should be encouraged to complete their MMR vaccination course if they have not done so already.

The NHS England Vaccination and Screening Directorate, via VDS, have commissioned NHS England’s Cohorting as a Service (CaaS) to identify patients in specific age-based categories as outlined in the Green Book chapters 21, 23 and 28.

CaaS will output a list of individuals that are eligible to be in the cohorts. Patient identifiable data will be flowed to the Data Processing Service (DPS) by CaaS. VDS may flow data which originates from these cohorts downstream as per separate DPIA/DSA. This data is to be used to drive invitation, and for delivering and monitoring the MMR vaccination programme, both the general MMR programme (running continuously) and with a specific catch-up campaign planned to run from 23 October 2023 to 31 August 2024.
